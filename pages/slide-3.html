Dataset profile

Million Song Dataset (MSD)

One million tracks of mostly Western popular music with high-level audio descriptors, song metadata, and artist similarity links—built as a benchmark for large-scale music information retrieval.

Scope & Coverage

  • 1M tracks spanning multiple decades of popular music with artist and album context.
  • High-level audio features such as key, mode, and energy plus song “hotness” scores.
  • Artist-centric signals: location, familiarity, and lists of similar artists for recommendation work.

Used for

  • Trend studies of keys, modes, and energetic profile changes across years.
  • Prototyping recommendation or similarity graphs with the built-in artist links.
  • Multimodal analyses when paired with lyrics (MXM), tags (Last.fm), or symbolic MIDI (Lakh).

Attributes

song_title artist_name year album key mode energy song_hotness artist_location artist_familiarity similar_artists

  • key: Musical key of the track, represented as an integer (0=C, 1=C#/Db, etc.).
  • mode: Binary flag indicating major (1) or minor (0) tonality.
  • energy: Perceptual measure of intensity and activity (0.0 to 1.0 scale).
