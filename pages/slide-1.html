Music to Culture

A single-page, scroll-snapped deck where slides and D3 visualizations live side-by-side.

Use ↑ / ↓ or click the dots on the right. Each slide is its own file, so you can work on them independently and still control things from the parent.

  • Fully modular: add, remove, or reorder pages in index.html.
  • Slide pages are clean HTML snippets.
  • D3 pages are standalone HTML (sandboxed via iframe).
