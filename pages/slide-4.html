Dataset profile

MusiXmatch Lyrics Dataset (MXM)

Official lyrics companion to the MSD: each track is expressed as bag-of-words counts over a 5,000-word vocabulary, making lyric-driven mood and topic analysis straightforward.

Scope & Coverage

  • Lyrics for MSD tracks in a sparse bag-of-words format.
  • Counts mapped to a fixed dictionary of the 5,000 most frequent terms.
  • Each row links back to the MSD track ID for easy joins with audio features.

Used for

  • Topic modeling or clustering to surface lyrical themes over time.
  • Sentiment, mood, or profanity filtering without needing raw text.
  • Lyrics-similarity search or blending with audio/tags for hybrid recommendations.

Attributes

msd_track_id word_index word_count vocab_size bag_of_words

  • word_index: Index of the word in the fixed 5,000-word vocabulary.
  • word_count: Number of times the word appears in the track's lyrics.
  • vocab_size: Total number of unique words in the vocabulary (5,000).
  • bag_of_words: Sparse representation of the lyrics as word counts.
